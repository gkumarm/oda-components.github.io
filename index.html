ODA SDKs

Open Digital Architecture (ODA) software development kits (SDK), for Rust, Golang, and Node.js, provide tools, libraries, and resources for developers to build applications or services based on the principles of ODA. These SDKs enable developers to interact with and leverage the capabilities of the ODA ecosystem, promoting interoperability and seamless integration.

ODA RIs

Open Digital Architecture (ODA) reference implementations (RIs) are built on our ODA SDKs and provide concrete and functional examples demonstrating best practices. Our open source RIs serve as a starting point for developers looking to build ODA compliant solutions.
